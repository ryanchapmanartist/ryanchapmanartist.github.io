Ryan Chapman

Artist and illustrator based in Portland, OR

Teaching

Ryan is currently an instructor at High Low Art Space, an artist-run studio providing drawing and painting lessons in the Portland area. You can sign up for his current classes here. In the past, Ryan has taught at several different art schools and specializes in teaching and spreading his passion for art to beginners of all ages. He also teaches private workshops in the area. To set up a workshop, please contact him at ryanchapmanartist@gmail.com.
